TREATMENT 1. Use a triple layer medical mask, discard mask after 8 hours of use or earlier if they become wet or visibly soiled.
2. Ensure cleaning of surfaces in the room that are touched often (tabletops, doorknobs, handles, etc.) with 1% hypochlorite solution.
3. Monitor temperature at regular intervals and check oxygen saturation with a pulse oximeter daily.
4. Isolate yourself in a well ventilated room, and the worn mask should be discarded only after disinfecting it with 1% Sodium Hypochlorite.
5. Frequent hand washing with soap and water for at least 40 seconds or clean with alcohol-based sanitizer.
6. Take rest and drink a lot of fluids to maintain adequate hydration. Follow a healthy diet, with negligible oil and spice
7. Connect with the treating physician promptly if any deterioration of symptoms is noticed.
8. Continue the medications for other co-morbid illness after consulting the treating physician.
9. Patients to follow symptomatic management for fever, running nose and cough, as warranted.
10. Patients may perform warm water gargles or take steam inhalation twice a day.
